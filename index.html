I was born and grew up in country side, then go to college in Ho Chi Minh City (the biggest city in Vietnam). The periodic of going to college is the most memorable to me. I met my best friends there who teach me how to cook, show me my mistakes if I do something wrong. we also share experience and story together. Even though we are far away from half of the earth, we still contact each other frequently. My friends and I volunteer at the areas where did not have electricity, people do not have clean water to drink, and children drop out. We walk from door to door encourage the children to go back to school. We also organize workshops about hygiene and sanitation.
